At Galvanic Game Studio, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users — both adults and children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you download and use our mobile applications available on both the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. By choosing to use our applications, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

01

Information Collection and Use

To provide and improve our services, we may collect certain types of information. This includes both personal information (which can identify you) and non-personal information (which cannot). For example, our apps may collect device-specific data such as device type, operating system version, crash reports, usage statistics, and interaction data to help us improve the user experience.

Some of our apps, particularly those designed for adults, may offer account login features through third-party providers such as Google or Facebook. If you choose to sign in this way, we may access basic profile information such as your name, email address, and profile picture. This information is used solely to personalize your experience within the app and is not shared with unaffiliated third parties.

Apps intended for children do not request or collect personally identifiable information. In compliance with applicable child privacy laws (such as COPPA in the US and GDPR-K in Europe), we take strict measures to avoid collecting, storing, or processing any data that can identify a child. Ads in kids' apps, if shown, are served only through child-safe, COPPA-compliant networks, and no behavioral or interest-based tracking is allowed.

Note: If a specific app requests location access, that app's Google Play Data Safety section will separately disclose whether precise or approximate location is collected and why.
02

Third-Party Services

Our applications may use third-party services that collect and process user data under their own privacy policies. These may include:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (for in-app advertising)
  • Firebase (for analytics and crash reporting)
  • Facebook (for login and analytics in adult apps)
  • Apple services (where applicable, on iOS platforms)

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers to better understand their practices.

03

Legal Bases for Processing Your Data

For users in the European Union (EU), the United Kingdom (UK), or Canada, we process personal data under the legal bases permitted by applicable data protection regulations, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and PIPEDA. These legal bases include user consent, performance of a contract, legal obligations, and legitimate business interests — such as preventing fraud, analyzing app performance, or improving functionality.

For child-directed apps, data is only processed when permitted under child data protection laws. For example, in cases where data collection is necessary to support internal operations of the app (like crash detection or session management), we ensure that no personally identifiable information is involved.

04

Social Logins (Adults Only)

Where social login is offered (e.g., "Log in with Google" or "Continue with Facebook"), the information obtained is limited to what is required for app functionality. This may include your name and email address and is used solely for account identification and personalization. We do not share this data with any other parties, and access is governed by the social platform's privacy settings.

05

Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as it is necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. When personal data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it. If data is stored in backups, it will be isolated and deleted as soon as possible.

06

Data Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, it's important to note that no internet-based service is ever completely secure, and while we do our best to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We recommend using secure networks and up-to-date devices when accessing our apps.

07

Children's Privacy

Galvanic Game Studio takes children's privacy seriously. We design our child-directed apps to comply with relevant child privacy protection laws. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without verified parental consent. If we discover that such data has been inadvertently collected, we take steps to promptly delete it.

For apps directed at children or used by users of unknown age, we do not transmit advertising identifiers or other device identifiers that could be used for behavioral tracking. Advertising shown in these apps is limited to contextual, non-personalized placements served through child-safe networks.

Parents and guardians have the right to review and request the deletion of any data potentially collected from their child. If you believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at appixgame@gmail.com.

08

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights related to your personal information, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict how your data is processed. If we rely on your consent to process your information, you may also withdraw that consent at any time.

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and to request its deletion. We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at appixgame@gmail.com. We will respond to such requests in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

09

Do-Not-Track Signals

Our services do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers, as no uniform standard has been adopted. We will update this policy should such a standard be established in the future.

10

Third-Party Links

Our apps may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those websites. We strongly encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you interact with.

11

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app or on our official website with an updated "Effective Date." For major changes, we may also notify you through email or app notifications where possible. We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed.

12

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, feel free to reach out.
